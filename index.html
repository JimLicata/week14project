James Licata's Project 4 Landing Page

Game 1

Very simple game of just collecting monsters from made from a simple canvas tutorial. Use the arrow keys to move and collect monsters.

Game 2

Slightly more complex take on game 1. All sprites have been replaced and now the space bar must be used to attack the monsters instead of simply colliding with them.

Game 3

Considerably more complex than both game 1 and 2. Now the monster moves around and can damage the player. Move around and attack with space like before, but now there is also a chance that the monster can drop a heart upon defeat to replenish one life point. The game ends when the player runs out of life.
